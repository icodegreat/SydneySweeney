Sydney Sweeney

I am an American actress born on September 12, 1997, in Spokane, Washington. I began my acting career in 2009 with a small role in the TV series "90210". I rose to prominence with her role as Cassie Howard in the hit HBO series "Euphoria" in 2019. I appeared in other popular TV shows such as "The Handmaid's Tale" and "Sharp Objects", as well as movies like "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood" and "Nocturne".
